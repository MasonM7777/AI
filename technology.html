Grammarly is useful because it helps improve writing by identifying and correcting grammar, spelling, punctuation, and style errors that can be easily overlooked. Whether you're writing an email, report, or essay, Grammarly ensures that your text is polished and professional. It goes beyond basic corrections, offering suggestions for more concise or engaging phrasing, making your writing clearer and more impactful. For non-native English speakers or those who struggle with writing, Grammarly provides invaluable assistance by offering real-time feedback, allowing users to learn and improve as they write.
